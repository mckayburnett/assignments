What is Yerba Maté?

Yerba Maté gives you the boost of coffee with the health benefits of herbal tea. It is a plant from the Holly family native to South America. Once harvested at the right age, the leaves and stems are toasted and ground into tiny pieces (seen below). Traditionaly it is consumed by steeping the ground leaves and stems in hot water and sipped through a filtered straw. But, you can drink it by brweing, french pressing, tea bags, and more!
